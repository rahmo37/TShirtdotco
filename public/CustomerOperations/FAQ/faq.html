Frequently Asked Questions

1. What categories of products do you offer?
We offer a wide range of products including T-shirts, Graphic T-shirts, Plain T-shirts, Accessories, Hoodies, and Sweatshirts.
2. Can I cancel my order?
Yes, you can cancel your order very easily, even if the order has shipped, but once delivered, no other changes can be made
3. How can I chat with a live agent?
Click on the "Chat with Live Agent" button at the bottom of this page to connect with a customer service representative.
4. Do you offer discount codes?
Yes! Use code DISCOUNT5 at checkout to receive a 5% discount on your purchase.
5. Is there free shipping available?
We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50 within the continental United States.
6. How can I check my order status?
Log in to your account and navigate to the "Order" section to view the current status of your orders.
7. How do I view my order history?
Your order history is available in your account dashboard under the "Order History" tab.
8. What payment methods do you accept?
We accept VISA, MasterCard, Maestro, and American Express for all purchases.
9. When will your app be available?
Our mobile app is coming soon! Stay tuned for updates and exclusive app-only offers.
10. What are your business hours?
Our business hours are from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, Monday through Saturday.
11. Can I modify products in my cart?
Yes, you can add, remove, or adjust quantities of items in your cart before completing your purchase.
12. Do you offer graphic T-shirts?
Absolutely! We have a diverse collection of graphic T-shirts featuring various designs and themes.
13. Do you sell hoodies and sweatshirts?
Yes, our catalog includes a range of comfortable hoodies and sweatshirts for all seasons.
14. How do I apply a discount code?
You can enter your discount code during the checkout process in the designated field.
15. Do you ship internationally?
Currently, we ship within the United States only, but we are working on expanding our shipping options.
16. How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Expedited shipping options are also available at checkout.
17. Can I track my shipment?
Yes, you can easily check your most up-to-date order status by clicking the order's section in the navigation bar
18. How do I contact customer support?
You can reach our customer support team via email at tshirtdotco@example.com or by calling (789)-159-7530.
